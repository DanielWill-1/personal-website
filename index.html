About

Architecting Intelligence.

I am Daniel Velladurai, an AI Researcher and Engineer operating at the intersection of theoretical machine learning and scalable infrastructure. My work is dedicated to advancing the capabilities of large-scale foundation models while ensuring their deployment is efficient, robust, and aligned with human intent.

With a foundation built on rigorous academic inquiry and honed through high-stakes engineering challenges, I approach artificial intelligence not merely as a set of algorithms, but as complex systems that require principled design. I believe the next leap in AI will come from closing the gap between raw computational power and elegant, interpretable architectures.
