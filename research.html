Advancing Clinical Intelligence through Precision AI.

My research sits at the intersection of deep learning and clinical application. I focus on developing robust, interpretative models that don't just achieve high accuracy on benchmark datasets, but provide actionable, transparent insights for medical professionals in high-stakes environments.

Core Focus Areas

Medical AI

Developing specialized architectures for multi-modal clinical data (EHR, genomics, imaging) to improve early detection and prognostic stratification in oncology and cardiology.

Multi-modal Transformers

Computer Vision

Pushing the boundaries of semantic segmentation and anomaly detection in high-resolution medical imaging (MRI, CT, WSI) with minimal annotation requirements via self-supervised learning.

Self-Supervised Segmentation

Explainable AI (XAI)

Moving beyond post-hoc attribution methods. Designing models that are inherently interpretable by embedding causal reasoning and mechanistic transparency into the training objective.

Causality Interpretability
